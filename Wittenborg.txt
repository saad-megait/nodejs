📋 PROGRAM OVERVIEW
- Official Degree: Master of Science (MSc) in Business Management
- Specialization: Applied Artificial Intelligence
- Total Duration: 18 Months (1.5 Years)
- Academic Workload: 90 ECTS (ECs)
- Language: 100% English-taught
- Location: Apeldoorn Campus Only (Netherlands)

💰 TUITION FEES & FINANCIALS
- Total Tuition Fee: €21,300 for the full 18-month program
- Primary Scholarship: Wittenborg Holland Scholarship (One-time €5,000 waiver)
- Net Cost (With Scholarship): €16,300
- Visa Support: Fully managed and sponsored by the university registry team

📅 FLEXIBLE INTAKES
- 24 August 2026 (September Block)
- 12 October 2026 (October Block)
- 30 November 2026 (December Block)

🧠 RELEVANT ENTRY REQUIREMENTS
- Academic Suitability: Open to science and technology backgrounds (fits MCA)
- English Language: IELTS minimum overall score of 6.5 (or TOEFL equivalent)
- Selection Process: Document review, written Motivation Letter, and video interview

📚 SYSTEMATIC SYLLABUS
- Semester 1: Marketing Management, Globalisation, Micro Economics, Ethics in Digital Technologies, Artificial Intelligence in Business
- Semester 2: Financial & Strategic Management, The World Economy, Applied Artificial Intelligence, Digital Security & Crisis Management
- Semester 3: Professional-Based Learning (Work Placement/Internship) and Final Dissertation

💼 WORKING RIGHTS DURING & AFTER STUDY
- Part-Time Work Limit: Max 16 hours per week during term / Full-time in summer
- Legal Baseline Wage: €14.71 per hour gross minimum (+ 8% holiday allowance)
- Post-Study Work (PSW): 1-year unrestricted Orientation Year (Zoekjaar) permit
- Spouse Policy: Allowed to join as visa dependent with matching 16-hour work limit
